Projects

Website Redesign for Accessibility and Engagement

Collaborated with a cross-functional team of five designers through Develop for Good to redesign a nonprofit’s educational website. Focused on improving cultural inclusivity, accessibility, and visual clarity using Figma for prototyping and Wix for implementation, resulting in a more intuitive and visually cohesive online experience for students and educators.

Streamlining Course Enrollment for Students

Partnered with six peers to reimagine the UCI WebReg system with a modern, student-centered interface. Conducted usability testing, built wireframes and interactive prototypes in Figma, and designed features to simplify course search, scheduling, and registration, enhancing usability and reducing navigation friction for university students.

Empowering Students Through Medical Insurance Literacy

Led the design of a website aimed at increasing medical insurance awareness among UCI students. The platform includes resource pages, an easy-to-navigate glossary, and an integrated chatbot to answer common questions. Focused on accessible information architecture and empathetic UX writing to support informed healthcare decisions.
